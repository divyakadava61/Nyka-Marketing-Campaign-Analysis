# Nyka Marketing Campaign Analysis

This repository contains a comprehensive analysis of Nyka’s marketing campaign performance, spanning the period from 2024 to 2025. The project covers the full data lifecycle—from initial cleaning and preprocessing to interactive visualization and strategic insight generation.

## Why This Project?

The primary objective was to evaluate Nyka's marketing efficiency by identifying which strategies, channels, and customer segments drive the most value. By analyzing 55,555 unique campaign records, this project provides the data-driven foundation needed to improve revenue, optimize marketing spend, and increase overall return on investment (ROI).

## How It Was Done

The analysis was performed using a combination of industry-standard data tools and rigorous preprocessing techniques:

-	Data Cleaning & Preprocessing: Used Excel and Power Query to handle null values, remove duplicates, and ensure data reliability.
-	Feature Engineering: Standardized date and currency fields and created new features like Year, Month, Quarter, and Marketing Spend to enable time-series analysis.
-	Granular Analysis: Split the channel_used column into multiple rows to perform detailed, channel-specific tracking.
-	Interactive Reporting: Developed two Power BI dashboards:
--	Dashboard 1: High-level performance overview featuring total revenue, ROI KPIs, and revenue trends.
--	Dashboard 2: Deeper analytical views, including acquisition cost vs. ROI scatter plots and conversion funnel analysis.

## Key Insights

The data revealed several critical performance trends and demographic highlights:

-	Financial Impact: Total revenue generated reached $28.7 billion, with a total ROI of $150.8K.
-	Growth Peaks: A significant surge in revenue was observed between July 2024 and May 2025.
-	Top Strategies: Influencer, Social Media, and Paid Ads consistently emerged as the high-performing campaign types.
-	Channel Reach: YouTube was identified as the most effective channel for maximizing impressions and clicks.
-	Customer Segments: Working women and college students were the highest revenue-generating groups.
-	Optimization Needs: Conversion funnel analysis identified significant drop-offs, highlighting a need for better targeting and onboarding.

## Strategic Recommendations

Based on these findings, the following actions are recommended to optimize future marketing efforts:

-	Prioritize High-Growth Channels: Increase investment in Influencer marketing and YouTube strategies to maximize reach and revenue.
-	Campaign Benchmarking: Use successful campaigns like NY-CMP-13007 and NY-CMP-22689 as blueprints for future executions.
-	Tailored Segment Strategies:
--	Engage working women through SEO and social media.
--	Target college students through influencer campaigns and Facebook.
-	Reduce Bottlenecks: Improve targeting and onboarding processes to minimize conversion funnel drop-offs.

**Tools Used**: Excel, Power Query, Power BI.
